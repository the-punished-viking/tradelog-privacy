TradeLog Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

TradeLog (“the App”) is developed and operated by TradeLogic Studios (“we”, “our”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the TradeLog mobile application.

1. Overview

TradeLog helps users record diary entries, create invoices, log mileage, and store receipts. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency regarding how your data is handled.

2. Information We Collect

A. Data You Create

  • Invoices, diary entries, mileage logs, and notes — stored only on your device.
  • Receipt photos and exported files (PDF/CSV) — generated and saved locally on your device. These are never automatically transmitted to us or any third party.

B. Data Collected Automatically

  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymized app usage data (screen views, interactions, performance metrics).
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash logs and diagnostic data to help identify and fix issues.
  • Google AdMob — displays ads and may use the Advertising ID to provide, measure, and personalize ad content.
  • Play Billing (Subscriptions & Purchases) — handles in-app subscriptions and payments through Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment details; all billing data is processed and secured by Google in accordance with their Payments Privacy Policy.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent any personal information. The following third parties may process limited, anonymized data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (Subscription and payment processing)

These services collect and process data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All locally stored data (invoices, photos, diary entries) remains on your device’s storage.
  • Data transmitted to Google (for analytics, crash reports, billing, or ads) is encrypted in transit using HTTPS (TLS).
  • We do not maintain personal accounts or cloud backups, so uninstalling the app will permanently remove your local data.

5. Advertising and Cookies

TradeLog uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve and measure personalized or non-personalized ads. Users in the EU/EEA, UK, or California may receive a consent prompt allowing them to opt out of personalized advertising.

6. GDPR (European Union & United Kingdom)

If you are located in the EEA or the UK, you have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Withdraw consent for personalized advertising.

Since TradeLog does not maintain user accounts or cloud data, most control remains on your device. For data handled by Google, please review their GDPR Compliance Center.

7. CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

California residents may:

  • Request disclosure or deletion of data collected by Google services.
  • Opt out of the sale or use of personal data for advertising.

TradeLog does not sell user data. For details, see Google’s CCPA resource.

8. Children’s Privacy

TradeLog is intended for users aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can delete local data at any time by removing entries or uninstalling the app.
  • Manage personalized ads via Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Reset your Advertising ID via Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete Advertising ID.

10. Third-Party Links

The app may contain external links (e.g., PDFs or resources). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last updated” date will always reflect the latest version. Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised terms.
